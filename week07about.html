Portuguese Water Dog

The Portuguese Water Dog is a loyal, lively, water-loving dog.

Health Concerns

  • Hip Dysplasia

  • Progressive Retinal Atrophy

  • Juvenile Dilated Cardiomyopathy

  • Storage Disease

The Portuguese Water Dog is an ancient breed. It is thought that the modern dog shares the same genetic pool with poodles, with distinctions in personality, appearance, and temperament.

Peekapoo

These dogs are loyal and affectionate and can be quite protective of the people they love.

Health Concerns

  • Progressive Retinal Atrophy

  • Legg-Calve-Perthes Disease

  • Patellar Luxation

  • Hip Dysplasia

The Peekapoo is not a purebred dog. It is a cross between the Pekingese and the Poodle.
